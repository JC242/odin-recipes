Hot dog

Description

hot dog is a food consisting of a grilled or steamed sausage served in the slit of a partially sliced bun. The term hot dog can refer to the sausage itself.

Ingredients

  • Slice buns
  • Sausage
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Put the sausage on the grill and wait until it has good color
  2. Put the sausage on the bun
  3. Top it with mustard
