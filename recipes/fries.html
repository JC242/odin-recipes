Fries

Description

Fries are deep-fried potatoes of disputed origin from Belgium or France. They are prepared by cutting potatoes into even strips, drying them, and frying them, usually in a deep fryer.

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slices the potatos into strips
  2. Soak the potatos in water to remove excess starch and dry them
  3. Fry them in oil
  4. Season with salt
